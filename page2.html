Why this is an Issue?

In today's music industry, we are quick to praise artists for their songs. However we usually don't give any props to the producers, who are responsible for creating most of a song. Sometimes producers don't receive pay and get their beats/songs stolen by artists who use it for their own personal gain. Addtionally songs are usually judged by critics and the public by the "sound" of the song, not the content of lyrics. Without good production, an artist won't be able to release songs that people would be listening to.
